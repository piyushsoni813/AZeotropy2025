About AZeotropy
AZeotropy is an interesting blend of various intriguing competitions, lecture series, panel discussions, exhibitions, workshops, and many fun-with-learning events. We are a non-profit organization aimed to bridge the gap between students, industry, academia, and promote it at a national level. To fulfill its promise to encompass the whole field of chemical engineering, AZeotropy has been working toward its purpose ever since its inception! We are excited to conduct all the competitions and events in offline mode after 2 years.
About Campus Ambassador Program
AZeotropy gives you a chance to showcase your leadership and organizational skills through our CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM. Campus Ambassadors represent AZeotropy in their respective colleges and their responsibility is to connect with the students who are enthusiastic to participate in various events, competitions and workshops conducted by AZeotropy. Registrations of students from all colleges with programs in chemical, petrochemical, and petroleum engineering are welcome.

Perks

Why become a Campus Ambassador?

Ambassador of the year

We will recognize and appreciate all the effort that you will put in. One will be felicitated with the prestigious Ambassador of the year award for his/her exceptional work throughout the tenure.

Free workshops

Every CA can attend any 2 workshops that will be organised by AZeotropy (online) absolutely free of cost. Why not learn something valuable and interesting?

Certificates of Complition

Being actively indulged in AZeotropy, throughout the year gets you a Certificate of Organization

Appreciation

Bridge the gap between your college participants, authorities and team AZeotropy by the exchange of queries and information. Put your best efforts to publicize AZeotropy to end up in Top 10 and receive a Certificate of Appreciation.

Freebies

Top 10 Campus Ambassadors get free T-shirts as a souvenir to cherish the memories that will be made together.

Networking Opportunities

Create a network among your peers that will help build your character, build confidence and gain a perspective

Internship Opportunities

Gain the invaluable chance to collaborate with leading chemical enterprises, while immersing yourself in the wealth of knowledge shared by top-level executives.

Industrial Visits

Bridge the gap between the industry and you. Get a chance to visit the industries and learn from professionals.

Interpersonal Skills

Get ahead of the crowd by strengthening your communication and management skills

Carrier Guidance

Each Campus Ambassador will receive monthly career guidance and resume-building sessions aimed at enhancing their job prospects and professional development

Social Media Recognition

The most dedicated and top-performing Campus Ambassadors will be prominently featured on our official social media platforms, recognizing their exceptional contributions

Future Event Planning Role

Provide an exclusive opportunity for top ambassadors to join the organizing committee for next year's festival, offering leadership experience.

Be You. Be Unstoppable. Be an AZeotropy Ambassador.
Responsibilities

Your role as a campus ambassador


Publicity

Publicize AZeotropy and its latest activities in your colleges through innovative online solutions and social media.

Bring Participation

Bring in your college's involvement in various online AZeotropy events and competitions. The higher the number of participation from your college, the more benefits you will receive.

Conduct-Competitions

Work with the AZeotropy core team to organize competitions at your universities.

Familiarize Others

Familiarize participants from your college with the online environments being used and solve their technical issues.
